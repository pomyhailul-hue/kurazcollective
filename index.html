Who We Are

Empowering Seniors Through Knowledge and Community

For many older adults, the path to U.S. citizenship can feel overwhelming. Language barriers, limited access to technology, transportation challenges, complex requirements, and financial hardship often prevent seniors from completing the process.

Through culturally responsive education, one-on-one support, peer navigation, and community advocacy, Kuraz Collective helps elderly immigrants achieve citizenship, strengthen their independence, and deepen their connection to the communities they call home.

Our Mission

Kuraz Collective Inc. advances equity and opportunity by empowering elderly immigrants with the tools, knowledge, and support needed to achieve citizenship, access resources, and engage fully in civic and community life.

What We Do

Our Programs

Comprehensive, culturally responsive support designed specifically for elderly immigrant adults navigating the citizenship journey.

Elderly Citizenship Attainment Project

A comprehensive support program designed specifically for older immigrant adults pursuing naturalization.

  • Citizenship test preparation classes
  • Naturalization interview preparation
  • One-on-one application assistance
  • Translation and interpretation support
  • Community citizenship clinics
  • Application fee assistance for eligible participants

Senior Peer Navigator Program

Trusted community leaders trained to guide fellow seniors through every step of the citizenship process.

  • Understand eligibility requirements
  • Complete paperwork with confidence
  • Access community resources
  • Stay motivated throughout the process
  • Connect with culturally relevant support systems

Civic Participation for Seniors

Citizenship is only the beginning. We help elderly immigrants learn how to exercise their voice in civic life.

  • Vote and participate in elections
  • Engage with local government
  • Access public services
  • Advocate for issues affecting older adults
  • Build stronger community connections
